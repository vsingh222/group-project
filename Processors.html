Processors

Intel

Intel processors are among the best for all the gamers and editors alike. Below are some of our featured intel processors:

Intel Core i7-4790k

Price: $453.00

Codenamed "Devil's Canyon", this processor packs a punch with a 4Ghz
base clock speed and up to 4.8Ghz turbo making this processor perfect
for gamers and photo/video editors.

Intel Core i9-9900K

Price: $549.99

Codenamed "Coffee Lake", this processor is one of the best for gaming
with a base clock speed of 3.6Ghz and a turbo clock speed of up to 5Ghz
making this processor the ultimate gaming processor.

Intel Core i7-9700k

Price: $409.99

Codenamed "Coffee Lake", this processor brings in a base clock speed
of 3.6Ghz and a turbo clock speed of 4.9Ghz making this a great
alternative to the i9-9900K above.

Intel Core i7-8700K

Price: $369.99

Launched last year and codenamed "Cofee Lake", this processor has a base
clock speed of 3.7Ghz and a turbo speed of up to 4.7Ghz, this processor
is perfect for all the newest CPU-intensive games

AMD

AMD Processors are good CPU's for gaming and often low priced compared to the Intel processors above. Below are some of our featured AMD processors:

AMD Ryzen 3 1200

Price: $95.53

This processor has a base clock speed of 3.5Ghz and has 4 cores making it
a good processor for gaming and multitasking.

AMD Ryzen 5 2600X

Price: $229.00

This processor packs a punch with a total of 6 cores running at 3.5Ghz per
core making this a speedy processor for the price.

AMD Ryzen Threadripper 1950X

Price: $566.99

With a total of 16 cores, this processor lives up to its name and is perfect
for multitaskers and gamers. It also packs 3.5Ghz per core making this
processor a speedy choice.
